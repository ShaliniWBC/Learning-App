## Retro Activity — Start/Stop/Continue

### Agenda

| Time | Activity | Facilitator Prompt |
|------|----------|--------------------|
| 0–5 min | Welcome and ground rules | "This is a safe space. We focus on process, not people. All contributions are valued." |
| 5–10 min | Share sprint snapshot | Present the Sprint Snapshot data. Highlight notable changes from prior sprint. |
| 10–15 min | Review prior actions | Walk through the Prior Actions Review table. Acknowledge completed items. |
| 15–30 min | Individual reflection and grouping | "Write items on sticky notes for Start, Stop, and Continue. One idea per note. You have 5 minutes to write, then we shall group similar items." |
| 30–40 min | Discussion and dot voting | "Each person has 3 votes. Place dots on the items you believe are most important. Let us discuss the top-voted items." |
| 40–45 min | Agree action items | "For each theme, who shall own the action and by when? Let us be specific." |
